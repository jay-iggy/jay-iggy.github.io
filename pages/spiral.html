HOME PROJECTS RESUME

Spiral is a bullet hell boss rush game. A strange creature in an old lady's basement has been mutated by a wizard's spell. Face off against a continuously changing and evolving monster as you find yourself way in over your head.

Programming: Jay Iggy, Toby Wilensky, Spencer Dill, Micah Vargas
Audio: Elijah DelCastillo

Gunther

Gunther is one of the bosses I created for the game. Gunther is a giant shotgun that has come to life and crawls around like an inchworm while littering the arena with explosive shotgun shells.
